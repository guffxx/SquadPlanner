HOW TO USE:
- Select a map from the right -
- Draw arrow lines -
- Mark the map with the icons -
- Add your own text to the map -
- Erase the icons, lines, and text with the eraser tools -
- Right click to drag icons and text -
- Download the finished Map when complete -
